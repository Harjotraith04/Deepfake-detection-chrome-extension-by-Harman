About DeepfakeProof

DuckDuckGoose has developed DeepfakeProof to give internet users a suitable tool for detecting deepfakes. DeepfakeProof is a browser plugin that warns users when they visit a web page that contains deepfakes. Beside real-time deepfake detection, DeepfakeProof provides user with insight into where deepfakes are located.


About DuckDuckGoose

DuckDuckGoose is a Delft-based startup focusing on deepfake detection. We envision creating a digital environment where we can still believe what we perceive. Through advances in artificial intelligence and computer vision, we are making it possible to detect deepfakes in an explainable manner. Our technology is able of detecting deepfake pictures and videos with real-time speed, not by only classifying the input as fake or real, but also by explaining the reasoning behind its decision.

For more information please visit our website or get in touch with us.

  • On what websites does DeepfakeProof work?

    DeepfakeProof works on all websites, except image search pages of search engines.


  • Where can I find more information on the privacy policy of the plugin?

    Check our privacy policy here. The workflow of the inner workings of DeepfakeProof can be found here.


  • What information do you store?

    We save the following information:

    • Hash of all the images you encountered while the plugin was on, on which a deepfake check was performed.
    • URL of all the images you encountered while the plugin was on, on which a deepfake check was performed.
